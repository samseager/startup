    • About What To Watch

    What to Watch is an app designed to help groups of people make decisions on what to watch when it comes to movies or TV shows. The app's purpose is to streamline the process of making a group decision on what to watch by allowing each person in the group to vote on their preference. This can help prevent long and potentially frustrating discussions about what to watch, as the app provides a quick and easy way to come to a group consensus.

    The app allows the user to create a poll with a list of available movies or TV shows to choose from, and then share that poll with their group. Each member of the group can then cast their vote, and the app will tally the results to show which option has the most votes. The group can then make a decision based on the results, and avoid any arguments or disagreements that might otherwise arise.
